The homelab

A single NixOS box in an actual shed runs k3s and most of my digital life: Immich for photos, Jellyfin for films, Frigate watching the garden, Vaultwarden, Navidrome, Audiobookshelf, and an RTX 3090 that moonlights as an AI lab (ComfyUI, Ollama, and an artificial-life sandbox with no fitness function). One make rebuild raises the whole thing from bare metal. There's a fuller tour in the hexes on the homepage.
