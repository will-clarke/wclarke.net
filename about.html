About

I studied anthropology, moved into software in 2011, and stayed. Since then I've worked mostly on backends and internal tooling, remotely for the last several years, from Dorset.

Outside work I build the things in projects: browser games, emulators, and a homelab in the shed. Recurring interests: recursive puzzle mechanics, small emulators and interpreters, genetic algorithms, build tooling, static site generators, and cob houses.

Once, I counted twelve and a half years of commits. Every previous version of this site is preserved in the museum.
