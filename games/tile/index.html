How to play

  • tap an empty cell to stamp the shape - its glowing eye lands where you tapped.
  • tap a placed piece to turn it - it cycles through every way it fits at that eye.
  • hold a piece to dissolve it away.
  • Fill the whole region with copies of the one shape. Hue is orientation - a finished tiling reveals its pattern in colour.
  • ◉ lights up cells no piece can ever cover - a warning you have boxed yourself in.
  • Solve with zero dissolves for a clean gold dot.
