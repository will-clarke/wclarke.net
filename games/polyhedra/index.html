Polyhedra playground

Pick a seed, stack operators, drag to spin. The ? explains every button.

A from-scratch general Conway engine - nothing hard-coded per solid: sixteen operators, stellation as cells of the face-plane arrangement, and true star faces the operators compose on (try stellating the dodecahedron, then a: the dodecadodecahedron). The v1 playground still lives at /playground/polyhedra.
