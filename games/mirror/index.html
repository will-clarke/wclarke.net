How to play

  • stroke the board to draw a mirror line - horizontal, vertical or diagonal. Every lit cell reflects across it, and the light grows.
  • You are filling in the faint target constellation. Match it exactly to win.
  • Light only ever grows. A copy that lands outside the target turns pink and the frame blushes - undo and try another line.
  • A line through the centre of cells leaves those cells single; a line between cells duplicates everything. Copies that fall off the edge are lost.
  • par is the fewest mirrors the target needs - solve at par for a gold dot. Two mirrors make a turn; two parallel mirrors make a slide.
