Hello, I'm Will

I'm a software engineer in rural Dorset. I work on backends and tooling for a living, and on games, emulators and various half-finished toys for no good reason at all. This site is where the second sort lives.

Now

Writing Go and Ruby for a dispatching team, remotely, from a house made of mud with no central heating. Off the clock: a homelab in the shed, a 3D printer I keep tinkering with instead of printing anything useful, three small children, and the occasional game that compiles to your browser. Not looking for work; always happy to talk shop.
